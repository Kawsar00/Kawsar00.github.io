Biriyani

The Spicy Bengali Rice Cuisine

Biriyani is a dish made with highly seasoned rice and meat.

Biryani takes time and practice to make but is worth every bit of the effort. Long-grained rice (like basmati) flavored with fragrant spices such as saffron and layered with lamb, chicken, fish, or vegetables and a thick gravy.

Biryani has mild fragrance, sticky rice, distinct taste of rice with hot and tangy flavour along with soft meat or vegetables.
